**Summary (relevant to the ongoing thread):**  

This is a ~15-minute prophetic encouragement from Ellen McFarlane (May 18, 2026).  

**Core message:**  
“The villa is yours and it’s not for sale.”  

God has cultivated specific **inheritances** (places, positions, relationships, provision, influence, legacy) for your life long before you arrived. These cannot be unlawfully taken, negotiated away, or sold off — even under pressure from “Ahab/Jezebel” spirits or worldly offers.  

It draws from **1 Kings 21** (Naboth refusing to sell his ancestral vineyard) and **Psalm 16:5-6** (“The lines have fallen to me in pleasant places; yes, I have a good inheritance”).  

Key themes:  
- Stand firm — you didn’t sell out when it cost you.  
- A shift into “cultivated blessing” — flourishing, generational places prepared intentionally by God.  
- Breaking squatter/illegal occupation off what’s rightfully assigned to you.  
- Restoration of confidence in your portion.  

It’s a strong “hold your ground, possession is coming” word that aligns with themes of anchoring, inheritance, divine assignment, and not yielding what Heaven has marked.  

Fits nicely with the Katoomba-to-Sydney transition energy if you’re sensing stewardship or “villa/estate” motifs in the spiritual/physical. Let me know if you want any specific parts expanded! 🙏
