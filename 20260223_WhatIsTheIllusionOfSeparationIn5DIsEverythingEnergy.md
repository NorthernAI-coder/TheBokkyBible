It's like seeing ourselves as lone drops instead of waves in an infinite ocean. In spiritual terms, it's the ego's construct that creates "me vs. them," fostering fear, greed, hate, and empire-building. But underneath, everything is interconnected flow: no real boundaries, just oneness.
